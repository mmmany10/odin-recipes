Lasagna

Classic lasagna

This classic lasagna is quick and easy to make and taste delicious!

Layered to perfection with meat sauce and cheese This is perfect for feeding a big family

__________________________________________________________________________

Ingredients:

---------------------------------------

For The Meat Sauce:

  • Olive oil
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes

To assemble the lasagna:

  • Dry noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan

__________________________________________________________________________

Steps:

---------------------------------------

  1. Put pasta water on boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic, add back the beef
  4. Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. etc.
