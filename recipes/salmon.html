Baked Salmon

This baked salmon is quick and easy to make and taste delicious!

Bursting with savory buttery lemon falvors.

__________________________________________________________________________

Ingredients:

---------------------------------------

  • Olive oil
  • Lemon
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Atlantic Salmon
  • Salt
  • Pepper

__________________________________________________________________________

Steps:

---------------------------------------

  1. Season Salmon with salt and pepper
  2. Squirt lemon over salmon
  3. Melt and drizzle butter over salmon
  4. Put in over to bake for 12 minutes
  5. etc.
